    How to cook the perfect pasta Italian-approved

    Follow our simple steps and enjoy the most delicious Italian tradition

    • Choose your favourite pasta from our great fresh selection.
    • Bring water to boil. 1.5 litres per serve and a teaspoon of salt.
    • In the meantime, bring that delicious sauce to simmer.
    • When the water is boiling, add the pasta.
    • Stir Gently and cook for 2 minutes to get the perfect pasta al dente.
    • Simmer the pasta in sauce for 20-30 seconds and add a drizzle of extra virgin olive oil.
    • Serve immediately with grated parmesan on top.
    • Enjoy!
